Hello, and thank you for checking out my personal portfolio!
I am an experienced project manager with a strong foundation in leading teams and collaborating with cross-functional groups to execute complex product development cycles. My expertise spans digital strategy, project management, product ownership, UI design, e-commerce, operations, client success, quality assurance testing and software engineering.
