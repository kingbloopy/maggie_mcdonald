Developer Projects

Michael Stars

Live Site

Michael Stars is a large e-commerce website for the LA based clothing brand. I worked on this project with three other developers to complete it's rebuild and launch in just a few months. Michael Stars utilizes Shopify's liquid templating language as well as a React and Tailwind frontend.

    Major components that I developed and contributed to:
  • Independently developed most of the home page such as all the image banners, sliders, and animations.
  • Built out a majority of the product page, including the image rendering and image zoom functionality, size swatches, accordian menus, and reviews.
  • On the category pages, I created the filters dropdown and sort-by dropdown menus.
  • Created the account login/sign up page.
  • On the user account page, I programmed most of the form submission logic for updating address information as well as designing layout of the page.

Faherty

Live Site

Faherty is another large e-commerce clothing brand. I worked on this project closely with one other developer to rebuild some components and maintain the website. Faherty utilizes Vue.JS and Shopify's liquid templating.

    Major components that I developed and contributed to:
  • Developed a majority of the category/collection pages, utilizing CMS templates for each category.
  • Worked on extensive swatch logic for the product page. The "All Day Shorts" in particular, required complicated logic.
