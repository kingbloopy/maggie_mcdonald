Brand Identity: Grandfolia

As the Design Director for Grandfolia, I had the incredible opportunity to bring the brand to life. I led the website design, created a custom logo, approved copywrite, and captured all the photography featured on the site. Working with a talented team, I directed and contributed to the creation of the floral arrangements showcased throughout the website. This project was a rewarding blend of design, artistry, and storytelling.
